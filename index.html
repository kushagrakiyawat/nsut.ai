Welcome to the NSUT.Ai

NSUT.Ai: Netaji Subhas University of Technology is a student-run, non-profit community of young researchers interested in the applications of Al and Data Science in a wide range of topics.

Our team

NSUT.Ai is established by young researchers from NSUT, working at the world's top research institutions like Harvard, Princeton, NUS, Vanderbilt and CMU.

AIRG NSUT is backed by Intel, and was established to promote research and enable interested individuals to become independent researchers by allowing them to initiate, collaborate and contribute to various research projects under the guidance of experienced researchers in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.
